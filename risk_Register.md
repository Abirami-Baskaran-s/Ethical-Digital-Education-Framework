Risk Register Overview – This risk register outlines key academic, operational, and system-related risks at Yoobee College, including inconsistent student performance, legacy SMS limitations, IT issues, staff retention challenges, and equity gaps for Māori and Pasifika students. It documents potential impacts, control measures, required training, and ongoing monitoring to support compliance, educational quality, and equitable outcomes.
